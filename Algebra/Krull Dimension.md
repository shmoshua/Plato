> [!definition]
> Let $R$ be a [[ring]].
> 1. The ***Krull dimension*** $\text{dim}R$ of $R$ is the maximum number $n\in\mathbb{N}$ s.t there is a chain of [[Prime Ideal|prime ideals]]: $$P_{0}\subsetneq P_{1}\subsetneq\dots \subsetneq P_{n}$$of length $n$ in $R$.
