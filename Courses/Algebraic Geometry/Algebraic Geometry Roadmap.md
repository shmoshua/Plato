##### 0.1.1 Properties of Varieties
> [!lemma] Lemma 1
> We have:
> 1. for any $S_1\subseteq S_{2}\subseteq K[x_{1},\dots,x_{n}]$, we have $V(S_{1})\supseteq V(S_{2})$.
> 2. for any $S_{1},S_{2}\subseteq K[x_{1},\dots,x_{n}]$, we have $V(S_{1})\cup V(S_{2})=V(S_{1}S_{2})$.
