#Series #Analysis 

We have that: $$\pi_{y}(x)=\frac{\braket{ x , y }}{\|y\|^{2}}y $$where $$\|y\|^{2}=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}}{6}$$and $$\braket{ x , y } =\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{(n+1)^{2}}=\frac{\pi^{2}}{6}-1$$Therefore, $$\pi_{y}(x)=\frac{\pi^{2}-6}{\pi^{2}}=1- \frac{6}{\pi^{2}}$$
