#Definition #DifferentialGeometry 

> [!definition]
> A surface $\Sigma \subseteq \mathbb{R}^{3}$ is a subset s.t. for all $p\in \Sigma$ there exists an open neighborhood $U\subseteq \mathbb{R}^{3}$ of $p$ s.t. 
> 1. there exists a smooth diffeomorphism $\phi:U\to \phi(U)\subseteq \mathbb{R}^{3}$ s.t. $\phi(U\cap\Sigma)=\phi(U)\cap(\mathbb{R}^{3}\times\{ 0 \})$
