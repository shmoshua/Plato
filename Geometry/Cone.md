#Definition #Geometry 

> [!definition]
> For $V\subseteq \mathbb{R}^n$, 
> 1. the ***cone*** generated by $V$ is defined as:
> 	$$\text{cone}(V):=\left\{   \sum_{v\in V}^{}\lambda_{v}v: \lambda_{v}\geq 0  \right\}$$
---
##### Properties
> [!lemma] Theorem (Caratheodory)
> Let $v^1,\dots,v^k\in \mathbb{R}^n$. For $x\in \text{cone}(V)$,
> 1. there exists non-negative $\lambda$ s.t. $x=\sum_{i}^{}\lambda_{i}v^i$ and $\left| \text{supp}(\lambda) \right|\leq n$
