#Definition #Optimization 

> [!definition]
> Let $A:X\to 2^X$. The ***$n$-th order Fitzpatrick function*** of $A$ is given as: $$F_{A,n}:X\times X\to \mathbb{R} \cup \{ \infty \},\quad (x,x^{*})\mapsto \braket{ x , x^{*} } +\sup_{\{ (a_{i},a_{i}^{*}) \}_{i\in[n-1]}\subseteq \text{gra }A}\braket{ x-a_{n-1} , a_{n-1}^{*} }  \sum_{i=1}^{n-2} +\braket{ a_{1} -x,  x^{*}}$$
