> [!definition]
> Let $\Omega:=\{ \pm 1 \}^n$ and $G$ be an [[graph|undirected graph]]. 
> 1. the ***MaxCut problem*** is the problem: $$x\in \underset{ x\in \Omega }{ \arg\max }\sum_{(u,v)\in E(G)}^{}\frac{1-x_{u}x_{v}}{2}$$
