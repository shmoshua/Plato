#Definition #AlgebraicTopology 
> [!definition]
> A [[topological space]] $X$ is ***acyclic***, if its [[reduced homology]] $\tilde{H}_{p}(X)=0$ for all $p\in \mathbb{Z}$.

^4e4b20

---
##### Examples
> [!h] Example 1
> We have that:
> 1. A point space is acyclic.
> 2. $\varnothing$ is not acyclic.
