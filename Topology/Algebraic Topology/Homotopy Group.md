#Definition #AlgebraicTopology 

> [!definition]
> Let $k\geq 1$. For a [[topological space]] $X$ and a base point $x_{0}\in X$, 
> 1. the ***$k$-th homotopy group*** is a group $\pi_{k}(X,x_{0}):=[(I^k,\partial I^k),(X,x_{0})]$ where $I^k:=[0,1]^k$ and $[(X,A),(Y,B)]$ denotes the set of homotopy classes $f:X\to Y$ where $f(A)\subseteq B$.
- **Remark**: If $k=1$, we have the [[fundamental group]].
