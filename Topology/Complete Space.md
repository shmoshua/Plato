#Definition #Topology 

> [!definition]
> Let $X$ be a [[topological space]] and $\mathcal{U}$ a [[uniform structure]] on $X$. Then, $X$ is ***complete*** if every [[Uniform Structure|Cauchy filter]] of $X$ converges. 
---
