#Definition #Topology 

> [!definition]
> A topological space $X$ is ***regular*** if:
> 1. for $x\in X$ and a closed set $A\not\ni x$, there exists open $U,V\subseteq X$ with $x\in U$ and $A\subseteq V$ s.t. $U\cap V=\varnothing$.
---
##### Examples
> [!h] Example 1 (Paracompact Hausdorff Space)
> We have that:
> 1. any [[Paracompact Space|paracompact]] Hausdorff space is regular.
