#Definition #Topology 

> [!definition]
> A [[topological space]] $X$ is ***second countable*** if $X$ has a countable [[Base of Topology|base]].
---
##### Examples
> [!h] Example 1
> $\mathbb{R}^n$ is second countable with: $$\mathcal{B}:=\{ B_{<r}(x):x\in \mathbb{Q}^n,r\in \mathbb{Q} \}$$ as basis.
---
